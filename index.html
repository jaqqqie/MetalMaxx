Located in Granbury Texas, we welcome you to our family owned and operated yard, MetalMaxx Recycling. We buy all ferrous and non-ferrous items including, copper, brass, junk cars, batteries, and more. Our scales are state certified and regulated, so rest assured you're getting paid on your true weights. Please take the time to like and/or follow us on Facebook at MetalMaxx Recycling LLC. There you will get the most up to date information or call us at (817) 279-1800. We will be happy to answer any questions you have.

Items we accept

  • COPPER
  • BRASS
  • METAL
  • ALUMINUM BREAKAGE
  • BATTERIES
