Located in Granbury Texas, we welcome you to our family owned and operated yard, MetalMaxx Recycling. We buy all ferrous and non-ferrous items including, copper, brass, junk cars, sheet tin, batteries, and more. Our scales are state certified and regulated yearly, so rest assured you're getting paid on your true weights. Please take the time to like and/or follow us on Facebook at MetalMaxx Recycling LLC. There you will get the most up to date information or call us at (817) 279-1800. We will be happy to answer any questions you have.

Items we accept

  • COPPER
  • BRASS
  • SHEET TIN
  • ALUMINUM BREAKAGE
  • BATTERIES
  • ALUMINUM
  • JUNK VEHICLES (TITLED ONLY)
  • SHORT & LONG IRON
  • ELECTRICAL WIRE
  • ALUMINUM CANS (BAGGED-NO FOIL)

Items we dont accept

  • ANY TYPE OF FENCING WIRE
  • BED/BOX SPRINGS
  • REBAR/ORNAMENTAL FENCE PANELS
  • RADIOACTIVE MATERIAL
  • FLAMMABLE FOAM FILLED DOORS/PANELS
  • STOLEN ITEMS
  • PROPANE/PRESSURIZED TANKS
  • BARRELS THAT CONTAIN(ED) HAZARDOUS MATERIALS
  • OILFIELD TANKS (UNLESS CUT INTO FOURTHS)
